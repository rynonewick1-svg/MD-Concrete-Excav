More About Us

MD Concrete & Excavation is Marly Donovan's business — built from the ground up on the Mid North Coast, one job at a time, on the belief that if you're going to pour it, you pour it properly.

Marly runs the business and the crew, backed on-site by his younger brother Sage. Because the team runs its own excavation gear alongside the concrete side, your site prep, levels and drainage are sorted by the same crew that pours and finishes your slab — no handballing to another trade, no gaps, no surprises on pour day.
