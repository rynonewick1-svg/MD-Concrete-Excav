MD Concrete & Excavation is Marly Donovan's business, named for exactly that — Marly Donovan Concrete & Excavation. Marly started it on the Mid North Coast the way most good trade businesses start: on the tools, job by job, with a simple rule — if you're going to pour it, you pour it properly.

Marly owns and runs the business, and he's still on-site for every job, not just running the books. He's backed by his younger brother Sage, who works alongside him on the crew — a genuine two-man family operation, not a name on a ute with subcontractors doing the actual work.

What sets the business apart is that it doesn't hand the dirt work to someone else. Marly's crew runs its own excavation gear alongside the concrete side, so site prep, levels and drainage are handled by the same team that pours and finishes the slab. One quote, one crew, one standard, from the first sod turn to the final trowel pass.

The business takes on residential and commercial work across Coffs Harbour and the Mid North Coast — everyday driveways and slabs through to fully decorative, architect-spec finishes — and treats every job, big or small, with the same attention to preparation and finish.
